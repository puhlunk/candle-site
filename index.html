Our Story

Oak & Ember began in a small Nashville workshop with a simple goal: create candles that transform ordinary moments into extraordinary experiences.

Every Oak & Ember candle is made by hand using natural soy wax, premium essential oils, and cotton wicks. We believe in creating products that are as beautiful as they are sustainable.

Our commitment to quality means we never use artificial fragrances or paraffin wax, ensuring a clean burn and authentic scent experience.

Our Process

Each candle is meticulously crafted through our signature process.

1

Source

We ethically source the finest natural ingredients, from American-grown soy to essential oils from around the world.

2

Blend

Our master chandler carefully blends fragrance profiles to create complex, layered scents that evolve as they burn.

3

Pour

Each vessel is hand-filled in small batches to ensure perfect consistency and quality control.

4

Cure

Our candles cure for two weeks, allowing the wax and scent to fully develop before reaching your home.

Our Materials

We believe what goes into our candles matters. That's why we use only premium, sustainable materials:

  • 100% Soy Wax - Renewable, biodegradable, and clean-burning
  • Cotton Wicks - Lead-free and zinc-free for a cleaner burn
  • Essential Oils - Pure, high-quality oils free from synthetic additives
  • Artisan Vessels - Handcrafted containers designed to be reused

Our commitment to sustainability extends to our packaging, which is 100% recyclable and made from post-consumer materials.
